Jianming Huang

CS Ph.D student
Huazhong University of Science and Technology

 


Biography:Jianming Huang is a Ph.D student in Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) advised by Prof. Yu Hua. He obtained the B.E degree in Computer Science from HUST in 2018.

He currently focuses on researching computer systems and memory architecture, including storage systems, non-volatile memory systems, and security.
